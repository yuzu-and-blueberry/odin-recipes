Make a banging bussing shin ramyun!

Need I say more? Shin Ramyun is a staple in any college student's diet. It's affordable, quick to make, and delicious in its own right. But every food can be taken to the next level by following just a few simple rules.

Ingredients

  • Shin Ramyun
  • 1 Egg
  • 0.5 Tablespoons Sesame Oil
  • 1 or 2 mushrooms of your choice
  • Cheese (shredded or grated)
  • Green onion

Instructions to make your noodles fantastic!

  1. You should have your ingredients prepped. Slice the mushrooms and green onions first. Grate the cheese if you are using block cheese.
  2. Fill a pot with enough water to just cover the noodles for it to cook. Bring water to a boil and cook for 3 minutes or until noodle texture is to your liking.
  3. I like my noodles spicy. Drain the water in the pot until it is barely covering the noodles.
  4. Bring to a boil and quickly add in the egg. Stir the pot while adding in your shredded cheese and mushrooms.Cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Pour eveything into a bowl. Add the sesame oil to the dish and mix it in. Garnish with green onion.
